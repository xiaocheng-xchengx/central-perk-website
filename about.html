About Central Perk

Gunther

Owner of Central Perk

Welcome! My name is Gunther, I'm the owner of Central Perk.

We are situated in New York City's Greenwich Village, in the same apartment block as Monica's apartment. We are 97 steps aways from Chandler and Joey's apartment, according to Joey.

You might see a beautiful, but horrible waitress walking around, doing nothing. Please don't be mad at her - that's my future wife - Rachel.

Our cafe occasionally holds music night. For more information about the songs, please visit Playbook.
